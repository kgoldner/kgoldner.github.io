Description: This group will investigate the topic of mechanism design with inequality and ordeals. Ordeals are payments (or allocations) that negatively impact the agents without transferring positive utility to the seller and are prominent in redistributive applications. The group assumes familiarity with algorithmic mechanism design and the basics of revenue maximization. We meet bi-weekly on zoom. Contact the organizers with any questions, concerns, interest.
